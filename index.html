      1. Booking & Confirmation

      All bookings must be confirmed in writing via email or through our official booking form. A deposit of 30% of the total tour cost is required to secure a reservation. The balance must be paid at least 30 days before the tour start date.

      2. Rates & Pricing

      All rates are quoted in Kenya Shillings (KES) or US Dollars (USD) and are based on per person sharing, unless otherwise specified. Prices are subject to change without notice due to fluctuations in exchange rates, changes in park fees, fuel costs, or government taxes. Single room supplements apply where requested.

      3. Payments

      Payments can be made via bank transfer, mobile money (M-Pesa: 0722233065), or credit card (additional charges may apply). A booking is only confirmed once payment has been received and acknowledged in writing.

      4. Cancellations & Refund Policy

      Cancellations must be submitted in writing. Charges apply as follows:

      • More than 30 days before departure: 20% of total cost
      • 15–29 days before departure: 50%
      • 7–14 days before departure: 75%
      • Less than 7 days: 100% (no refund)

      No refunds are provided for unused services, late arrivals, or early departures.

      Clients are responsible for their medical fitness to travel. Travel insurance with medical cover is strongly recommended. Certain vaccinations (e.g., yellow fever) may be required. Please notify us of any medical conditions or dietary restrictions in advance.

      8. Alterations by the Company

      We reserve the right to alter itineraries, accommodations, or transportation as necessary due to circumstances beyond our control. In case of force majeure (e.g., strikes, weather, political unrest), alternative arrangements will be made when possible.

      9. Client Responsibilities

      Clients must have valid passports, visas, and travel documents. They must adhere to local laws and customs and ensure the safety of their belongings.

      10. Children Policy

      Children under 12 may receive discounted rates depending on the tour or accommodation. They must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

      11. Complaints & Disputes

      Any complaints must be reported immediately to the tour guide. If unresolved, they must be submitted in writing within 14 days after the tour.

      12. Governing Law

      These terms are governed by the laws of the Republic of Kenya.
